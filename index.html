Academic Profile

I am with the Graduate School of Information Science and Technology at The University of Tokyo, and also affiliated with the Department of Computer Science, the Department of Information Science, the National Institute of Informatics, and the Japanese-French Laboratory for Informatics.

Research

Current Work

Recent work focuses on privacy-preserving graph analysis, approximation algorithms, rank aggregation, sparse vector publication, and optimization methods for emerging computational models.

  • Communication-Efficient Publication of Sparse Vectors under Differential Privacy via Poisson Private Representation
    AsiaCCS 2026, accepted
  • Approximation Algorithms for the b-Matching and List-Restricted Variants of MaxQAP
    ISCO 2026, accepted
  • Improved Differentially Private Algorithms for Rank Aggregation
    AAAI 2026
